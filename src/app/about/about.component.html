A PICTURE A DAY

365 photo project by Kilchenmann

To improve my photograph skills I started this project. The challenge is to take a picture a day during at least one year = 365 days. The project starts on May 1st 2019.

I hope you have just as much fun looking at the photos as I have with creating them.
